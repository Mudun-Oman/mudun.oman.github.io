About Us

Bahjat Andaam Trading is established in Sultanate of Oman. The company aims for longer and closer working relationships with our clients and suppliers. We not only provide immediate and reliable services but continually exceed expectations and goals.e are involved in trading of various materials for Oil and Gas, Civil Construction, Petrochemical, Electromechanical and other Industries from reputed manufacturers. We are providing our services to almost all the major Industrial and drilling companies in Oman.
